Tableau Visualization

This is a project about sales of Adidas in the USA using different visualization under regional and product perspective. The dataset shown was extracted from Kaggle, illustrates sales records in 2020-2021. The first step before visualization was data cleaning. I used Power Query to ensure that the data I worked with was accurate. My goal was creating an easily understandable and representable view of this data using graphs, charts and map tools.

Vehicle recalls

Visualization of Vehicle Recalls in the USA 1966-2023 dataset extracted from Data.gov.
I experienced plenty of missing, incomplete or incorrectly formatted and/or redundant data within the dataset. Thus, data cleaning was necessary. Power BI Charts prove to be the powerful tools to pull off a meaningful and invaluable dashboard. We can get the desired information easily and quickly provided via filters, whether it is a specific year or category of recalls.
